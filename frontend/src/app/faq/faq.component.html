FREQUENT ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)


How can I borrow books from the library?
faq

To borrow books, you normally must be a library member. Once you have a library card,
you may check out books at the circulation desk or using a self-checkout machine.


How to return the books?
faq

To return books, you can drop out the circulation desk or using a self-checkout machine.
What is the usual loan period for borrowed books?
faq

The loan period can vary, but it's commonly around two to four weeks.
Some libraries offer extended loan periods for certain materials or for members with special privileges.
Can I renew the books I've borrowed
faq

Yes, most libraries allow you to renew books unless another patron has
placed a hold on them. Renewals can often be done online, over the phone, or in person.
